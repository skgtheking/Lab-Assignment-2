My Portfolio

My portfolio is a reflection of my journey, a testament to my growth as a computer science student specializing in game development at the University of Idaho. It represents not just the culmination of my academic endeavors, but also my unwavering passion for gaming and the countless hours I've poured into honing my skills.

The first section of my portfolio showcases my academic achievements. From my early coursework in programming fundamentals to the more advanced topics in game design and development, I've documented my progression through detailed project descriptions, code snippets, and personal reflections. This section serves as a record of my commitment to learning and mastering the foundations of computer science.

The heart of my portfolio lies in the game development projects I've undertaken. Each project is a labor of love, a fusion of creativity and technical expertise. I've included detailed breakdowns of the games I've developed, from concept to execution, highlighting the challenges I faced and the solutions I devised. These projects demonstrate my ability to transform ideas into tangible, interactive experiences, ranging from 2D platformers to immersive 3D worlds.

In the third section, I showcase my involvement in extracurricular activities related to game development. Whether it's participating in game jams, attending gaming conferences, or collaborating with fellow students on innovative projects, I've documented my contributions to the gaming community. This section illustrates my dedication to continuous learning and my commitment to staying updated with industry trends.

The fourth part of my portfolio delves into my unique perspective as an Indian student in the field of game development. Drawing from my cultural heritage, I've explored ways to infuse Indian themes and storytelling elements into my game projects. This section emphasizes my desire to bring diversity and fresh perspectives to the gaming world, showcasing the potential for culturally inspired games to resonate with a global audience.

Lastly, my portfolio highlights my vision for the future. It's not just a collection of past achievements but a roadmap for what's to come. I outline my aspirations to contribute to the gaming industry by pushing the boundaries of game design, storytelling, and technology. My portfolio is a dynamic document, evolving alongside my skills and ambitions as Shubham Gupta, a passionate junior computer science student, and aspiring game developer.
