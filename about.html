About ME!!!

Hello, I'm Shubham Gupta, a junior computer science student currently pursuing my studies at the University of Idaho. Originally hailing from the bustling city of Kolkata, India, I've always been deeply passionate about the world of gaming and game development. It's this very passion that led me to choose game development as my specialization within computer science.

From a young age, I've been captivated by the immersive worlds and intricate mechanics of video games. I've spent countless hours exploring virtual realms, and it's this enthusiasm that has driven me to delve deeper into the art and science of creating games. Through my coursework and personal projects, I've been honing my skills in programming, game design, and the technologies that power the gaming industry.

But my journey extends beyond the classroom. I actively seek opportunities to apply my knowledge and collaborate with fellow students on exciting game projects. Whether it's attending gaming conferences or participating in hackathons, I'm always on the lookout for ways to expand my horizons.

As I continue my education and gain more hands-on experience in the field, I'm excited to contribute my creativity and technical expertise to the world of game development. My name is Shubham Gupta, and I'm on a mission to turn my passion for gaming into a fulfilling and successful career.

My journey as a junior computer science student at the University of Idaho, hailing from Kolkata, India, has been a thrilling exploration into the world of gaming and game development. With a specialization in game development, I've immersed myself in the art of creating immersive gaming experiences, combining my technical prowess with my innate passion for gaming. Every line of code and every game project I undertake brings me closer to realizing my dream of contributing unique, culturally inspired games to the industry. As Shubham Gupta, I'm eager to continue this exciting journey of growth, creativity, and innovation in the ever-evolving realm of game development.
